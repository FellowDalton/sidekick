# Harpenden family cooling-off

Durable shortlist for hot-weather family outings near Harpenden.

## Closest shaded parks

- **Batford Springs Local Nature Reserve** — strong close-to-home shaded/cool option: chalk stream, River Lea, reed fen, wet woodland, meadow, woodland boardwalk, riverside path, play park and picnic benches. Council specifically says **do not swim in the water**.
- **Rothamsted Park** — best all-round Harpenden park: 20.13 ha with play areas, woodland/wildlife habitats, nature trails, benches, car park, skate park, sports facilities and paths to the leisure centre. Shade comes from mature oaks, ancient hedgerows, pockets of woodland planting and the mature lime avenue.
- **Lydekker Park** — small central green space with open grass, mature trees, shrub beds and wildlife ponds; good for a shorter shaded stop rather than a water-play destination.
- **Harpenden Common** — large 94 ha common with woodland and scrub; useful for shade/walking but less targeted at splash/pool play.

## Water play / splash pads

- **Verulamium Splash Park, St Albans / Abbey View** — closest named splash park found; Everyone Active result lists it as “Verulamium Splash Park” and gives **01727 736080**. Pair with **Verulamium Park**, a 175-acre Green Flag / Green Heritage park, for shade/space around the water-play trip.
- **Cassiobury Park paddling pools, Watford** — best-featured free splash option: Watford Council says the paddling pools are free, suitable for kids of all ages/abilities, and include two splash pools, splash pads, water jets/fountains, changing facilities, toilets and a cafe kiosk.
- **Gadebridge Splash Park, Hemel Hempstead** — another splash-park option surfaced in Dacorum/Enjoy Dacorum search results; verify current opening before travelling.

## Pools / lidos

- **Harpenden Leisure Centre, Leyton Road AL5 2HU** — close indoor fallback. Swimming.org lists a 25m main pool (0.9m shallow / 1.8m deep), a learner/teaching pool (17.99m x 10.6m; 0.8m shallow / 2m deep), pool hoist and Learn to Swim lessons. Phone from search result/Poolfinder: **01582 767722**.
- **Hitchin Lido / Hitchin Swimming Centre** — best outdoor-pool outing. Visit Hitchin says it has a 50m heated outdoor pool open May–September with sunbathing terrace, plus indoor 25m pool, indoor toddler pool and Splash Café.

## Working ranking for a hot day

1. **Rothamsted Park + Harpenden Leisure Centre** for least travel: shaded park/play first, then indoor swim if heat peaks.
2. **Batford Springs** for a shaded nature/play picnic; keep it explicitly non-swimming.
3. **Verulamium Splash Park + Verulamium Park** for closest splash-pad-style outing.
4. **Cassiobury Park** for bigger free splash-pool facilities when willing to drive/train farther.
5. **Hitchin Lido** for a proper outdoor pool day, especially May–September.
